## Links

Just some links that I use, and may be of use for others. This is a
work in progress and the linkset will be extended in the future.

### Purely technical
* [SparNatural](https://sparnatural.eu/), a typescript only solution to make it easy for end-users to make selections on sparql end-points. 
* [Apache Jena](https://jena.apache.org/), and the database that I usually use: [Fuseki](https://jena.apache.org/documentation/fuseki2/index.html).
* [LinkedDataHub](https://atomgraph.github.io/LinkedDataHub/), a low code approach to visualisation and editing of linked data.


### Semantics in general
* [Sparna](https://www.sparna.fr/en/), a knowledge organisation company. they make the [SparNatural](https://sparnatural.eu/) solution.
* [Bob duCharme](https://www.bobdc.com/blog/), one the experts on semantics and rdf.
* [Justin Dowdy Weblog](https://github.com/justin2004/weblog/tree/master), a high technical and excelent weblog about RDF technology.
